PLATING IDEAS

Three ways
to use the tray.

01 · BREAKFAST

On eggs.

Soft scramble, sea salt, lemon zest, and a fistful right at the end. The pea shoots wilt just a little; the sunflower stays crunchy.

02 · LUNCH

On a bowl.

Farro, roasted squash, tahini-lemon, and a generous mound on top. The broccoli sulforaphane wakes up when you bite into it.

03 · ANYTIME

On toast.

Sourdough, smashed avocado, chili crisp, baller mix. The fastest possible way to look like you tried.
