WHY WE EXIST

Two broken systems.
One fix.

The American food system runs on distance. Lettuce travels 1,400 miles before a Chicagoan eats it. Fragile produce is picked early, sprayed, boxed, trucked, and refrigerated for days — losing nutrients at every stop.

Meanwhile, downtown Chicago has the highest office vacancy on record. Whole floors of beautiful buildings sit empty. Quiet. Useless.

FARMZER0 was built to fix both at once — turn the empty floors into farms, and the long supply chain into a 14-floor elevator ride.

Our farms feed the people in the same building, the hospital across the street, the catering company two blocks away. We measure success in feet, not miles. In hours, not days.
